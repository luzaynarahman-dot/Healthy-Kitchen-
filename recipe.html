Instructions

1

In a large bowl, combine the sliced meat with olive oil, yogurt, tomato paste, and all spices. Mix well until meat is thoroughly coated.

⏱️ Marinate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.
2

Preheat your grill or large pan to medium-high heat. Thread the marinated meat onto skewers if using.

3

Cook the meat for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until nicely charred and cooked through. Internal temperature should reach 165°F (74°C).

4

Warm the pita bread on the grill for 30 seconds per side.

5

Slice the cooked meat thinly. Serve on warm pita with fresh tomatoes, onions, and garlic yogurt sauce.

Chef's Tip: For extra flavor, add a pinch of sumac to the yogurt sauce!
